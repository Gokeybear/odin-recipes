Garlic Bread

Desciption

This garlic bread will be a tasty addition to any dinner! Full of roasted garlic flavor, parmesan cheese and fresh basil, this bread is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Fresh loaf of your favorite bread
  • 1/4 cup of butter softened
  • 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of freshly grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to broil and halve your bread.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix butter, garlic, salt, basil and parmesan. Spread mixture evenly on both halves of the bread.
  3. Place bread, butter side up, under the broiler for 5 minutes, or until lightly toasted.
