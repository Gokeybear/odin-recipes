Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic weekday meal that will leave your family wanting more. It is a delicious mix of creamy cheese, tangy tomato sauce and hearty vegetables to help warm you up on a cold winter day.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of fresh mushrooms
  • 3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 jars of marinara sauce
  • 1 handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 container of ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
