Avocado Toast

Description

This avocado toast is my go to morning breakfast! It is filling and full of healthy fats that help fuel your day. Sometimes I like to add a fried egg on top for more pizazz to my morning!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of crusty sourdough bread
  • 1 avocado
  • Everything bagel seasoning
  • Red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Feta cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Lightly toast your bread in the toaster.
  2. In a small bowl, smash your avocado, salt and pepper together.
  3. Spread avocado mixture over toast and add everything seasoning and red chili flakes to taste.
